CHIEN SHIUNG WU

“When women change the world, yet remain in the shadows”

Chien-Shiung Wu marked a turning point in physics through her experiment. She was not rewarded for her efforts and was pushed aside by her two colleagues.

RESEARCHER

“Born in China, a brilliant physicist, she made a major contribution to a key theory, but in 1957, the Nobel Prize was awarded to two men without acknowledging her.”

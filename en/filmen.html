The Film

Trailer

Discover the trailer of the film “Hidden Figures”, released in 2016.

Hidden Figures is a powerful film that tells the true story of three African-American women who worked at NASA during the 1960s.

At a time marked by segregation and inequality, these scientists proved their intelligence and determination. Thanks to their calculations, the United States was able to send a man into space.
The film shows how courage, hard work, and perseverance can change the course of history. Inspiring and moving, Hidden Figures encourages us to believe in our dreams and never give up.

Project completed in 2025 as part of an educational exercise at the MMI department of Montbéliard
